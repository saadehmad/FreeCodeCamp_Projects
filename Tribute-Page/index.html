Arfa Abdul Karim Randhawa

Youngest Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP)

Arfa Abdul Karim Randhawa was a Pakistani student and computer prodigy who, in 2004 at the age of nine, became the youngest Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP). She kept the title until 2008. Arfa represented Pakistan on various international forums including the TechEd Developers Conference. She also received the President's Award for Pride of Performance. A science park in Lahore, the Arfa Software Technology Park, was named after her. She was invited by Bill Gates to visit Microsoft Headquarters in the United States. Arfa died in hospital at Lahore on 14 January 2012, aged 16, She suffered a cardiac arrest.
